Our Story

Discover the passion behind Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals Ltd has grown from a small local operator to one of Uganda's most respected safari and transportation companies. Our journey began with a single 4x4 vehicle and a passion for sharing Uganda's incredible wildlife with visitors.

Today, we operate a fleet of modern safari vehicles and offer comprehensive tour packages across Uganda, Rwanda, Kenya, and Tanzania. Despite our growth, we've maintained the personal touch and attention to detail that characterized our early days.

Our Mission

To provide unforgettable African experiences through exceptional safari adventures and reliable transportation services, while promoting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and conserves wildlife.
