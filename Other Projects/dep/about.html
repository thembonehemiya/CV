About Pullman Safaris

Your trusted partner for unforgettable African adventures

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals Ltd has grown from a small local operator to one of Uganda's most respected safari and car rental companies.

What began as a passion project for wildlife enthusiasts has transformed into a full-service travel company specializing in creating bespoke safari experiences and providing reliable transportation solutions.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with local communities, conservation organizations, and hospitality providers across East Africa, allowing us to offer authentic experiences while supporting sustainable tourism.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide exceptional safari experiences and reliable transportation services while promoting sustainable tourism that benefits both our clients and the local communities we work with.

Our Vision

To be recognized as East Africa's premier safari and car rental company, known for our exceptional service, ethical practices, and unforgettable travel experiences.

Our Values

  • Commitment to sustainable tourism
  • Exceptional customer service
  • Safety and reliability
  • Support for local communities
