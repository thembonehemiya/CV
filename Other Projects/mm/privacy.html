Privacy Policy

Your data security is our priority

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals Ltd ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services or website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data:

  • Name, email, phone number for bookings
  • Passport details (for safari permits)
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Driver’s license (for car rentals)

Automated Data:

  • IP address, browser type
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Cookies (see Section 5)

Cookie Policy

We use the following types of cookies:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Website functionality (e.g., booking forms) Session
Analytics Visitor statistics (Google Analytics) 1-2 years
Preference Remember user settings 30 days

You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Process bookings and payments
  • Communicate about reservations
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal requirements (e.g., national park regulations)
  • Marketing (only with your consent)

3. Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary:

  • Service Providers: Hotels, guides, or car rental partners to fulfill bookings
  • Legal Requirements: Government authorities for permits or legal compliance
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateways (we don’t store credit card details)

We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Security Measures

We implement:

  • SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and notify us immediately of any suspicious activity.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (e.g., language)
  • Analyze website traffic via Google Analytics
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some website features may not work.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (where legally permitted)
  • Opt out of marketing emails

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@pullmansafaris.com.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
