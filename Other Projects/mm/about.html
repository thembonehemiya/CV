Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2010, Pullman Safaris began as a small tour operation with just two vehicles and a passion for showcasing Uganda's natural wonders. What started as a dream to share the beauty of our country with visitors has grown into one of Uganda's most respected safari and car rental companies.

Over the years, we've expanded our fleet, trained expert guides, and developed relationships with communities across Uganda's national parks. Our commitment to sustainable tourism and exceptional service has earned us recognition from both international travelers and local partners.

Today, we operate a diverse fleet of vehicles and offer safari experiences in all of Uganda's major national parks, while maintaining the personal touch that set us apart from the beginning.
