About Pullman Safaris

Your Trusted Partner for African Adventures

Our Story

Founded with a passion for showcasing Uganda's incredible natural beauty and wildlife, Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals Ltd has been connecting travelers with unforgettable African experiences for years.

We specialize in creating personalized safari adventures that allow you to witness Uganda's diverse ecosystems, from the misty mountains of Bwindi where mountain gorillas roam, to the vast savannas of Queen Elizabeth National Park.

Our comprehensive car rental services ensure you have reliable transportation whether you're embarking on a self-drive adventure or need comfortable transfers for business or leisure.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional safari experiences and reliable transportation services that showcase Uganda's natural wonders while promoting sustainable tourism and supporting local communities.

Our Vision

To be Uganda's leading safari and car rental company, recognized for our commitment to excellence, environmental conservation, and creating life-changing experiences for our clients.
