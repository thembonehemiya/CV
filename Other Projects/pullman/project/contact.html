Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Our safari packages typically include accommodation, meals, transportation, park entrance fees, professional guide, and all activities mentioned in the itinerary. Gorilla permits and other special permits are clearly indicated.

Yes, we offer airport transfer services from Entebbe International Airport to Kampala and other destinations. This can be arranged as part of your safari package or as a standalone service.

For car rental, you'll need a valid driver's license (international driving permit recommended), passport, and credit card for security deposit. Additional documentation may be required for long-term rentals.

We recommend booking safaris at least 2-3 months in advance, especially for gorilla trekking which requires permits. Car rentals can be booked with shorter notice, but advance booking ensures better vehicle availability.

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (MTN and Airtel), and cash payments. For international clients, we also accept wire transfers and payments through our secure payment partners.

While we don't directly provide travel insurance, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and adventure activities. We can recommend trusted providers.
