Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book my safari?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for gorilla trekking permits and peak season travel (June-September, December-January).

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop-off services for all our safari and car rental clients. This can be arranged as part of your package or as a separate service.

What's included in your safari packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, transportation, park fees, professional guide, and specified activities. Specific inclusions vary by package - contact us for detailed information.

Can I rent a car for self-drive safaris?

Absolutely! We offer well-equipped 4x4 vehicles perfect for self-drive adventures. All rentals include GPS, emergency contacts, and 24/7 support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and mobile money. A deposit is required to confirm bookings, with the balance due before travel.
