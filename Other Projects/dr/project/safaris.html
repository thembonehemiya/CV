Explore Uganda's Premier Destinations

From gorilla trekking to mountain climbing, discover the diverse landscapes and wildlife that make Uganda the Pearl of Africa.

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Mountain Gorilla trekking in this UNESCO World Heritage Site. Home to over half of the world's remaining mountain gorillas.

Activities: Gorilla tracking, birding, forest walks, Batwa cultural visits
Plan Your Visit

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Famous for tree-climbing lions and Kazinga Channel boat safaris. Diverse wildlife in stunning crater landscapes.

Activities: Game drives, boat cruises, chimp trekking in Kyambura Gorge
Plan Your Visit

Kibale Forest National Park

Chimpanzee capital of the world with 13 primate species. Perfect for primate enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Activities: Chimpanzee tracking, primate watching, birding, nature walks
Plan Your Visit

Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Snow-capped peaks and glacier hiking in Africa! The legendary Mountains of the Moon offer incredible trekking experiences.

Activities: Mountaineering, multi-day hikes, photography, cultural tours
Plan Your Visit

Lake Mburo National Park

Closest park to Kampala with zebras and antelopes. Perfect for walking safaris and unique wildlife experiences.

Activities: Walking safaris, biking, boat rides, horse riding
Plan Your Visit

Kidepo Valley National Park

Remote and untouched wilderness with spectacular landscapes. One of Africa's most pristine national parks.

Activities: Game drives, cultural tours (Karamojong), scenic landscapes
Plan Your Visit

Ssese Islands

Tropical paradise on Lake Victoria. 84 lush islands perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Activities: Beach relaxation, fishing tours, nature walks, biking
Plan Your Visit

Lake Bunyonyi

Uganda's most scenic lake, often called "the Switzerland of Africa." Perfect for relaxation after gorilla trekking.

Activities: Canoeing, island hopping, swimming, cultural tours
Plan Your Visit

Source of the Nile - Jinja

Historic source of River Nile and adventure capital of East Africa. Perfect for thrill-seekers and water sports enthusiasts.

Activities: White-water rafting, bungee jumping, boat cruises, quad biking
Plan Your Visit
