About Pullman Safaris

Your trusted partner for unforgettable African adventures

Our Story

Founded with a passion for showcasing the natural beauty and wildlife of East Africa, Pullman Safaris and Car Rentals Ltd has grown to become one of Uganda's most trusted tourism companies. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide authentic, safe, and memorable experiences that connect travelers with the heart of Africa.

Based in Kampala, Uganda, we specialize in creating personalized safari experiences that range from thrilling gorilla trekking adventures in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park to comprehensive wildlife tours across Uganda's pristine national parks. Our extensive knowledge of local cultures, wildlife patterns, and hidden gems ensures that every journey with us is unique and transformative.

Beyond safaris, we provide reliable car rental services for self-drive adventures, corporate travel, and special occasions. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for tourists, NGOs, and business travelers throughout East Africa.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional safari experiences and reliable transportation services that showcase the natural beauty, wildlife, and cultural heritage of East Africa while promoting sustainable tourism and contributing to local conservation efforts.

Our Vision

To be the leading safari and car rental company in East Africa, recognized for our commitment to excellence, environmental conservation, and creating transformative travel experiences that connect people with Africa's incredible natural heritage.
