Our Story

The government of Uganda has put in more effort to develop rural communities and as the NGO operating in the rural communities has been formed for the purpose of eco-tourism, natural environmental conservation and sustainable human development.

We members of Lhubiriha conservation and tourism organization (LUCTO) having been an organization, devoted ourselves and creating power by common conservation activities among members and large number of people within our area, thereby to announce the new law that shall guide and govern the group (NGO) in the smooth running of its plans and future activities.

Thereby as aware of the threats the degraded environment is exposing to the people of this area due to human activities and other natural hazards such as floods, landslides, prolonged drought and many others, reminding to awaken people's potentials to exploit sustainably the natural resources around them while reconciling the Rwenzori mountains potential resources and the community with modern scientific ideas.

Our Mission

To create awareness and promotion of the ecosystems along Lhubiriha basin and Rwenzori mountains for sustainable development.

Our Vision

A culture of tourism and relationship amongst the communities in the preserved environment on the Rwenzori Mountains.

Our Goal

The overall goal of LUCTO is to ensure that every community member knows the importance of environmental conservation and tourism.

Our Objectives

1

To establish important tourism sites, this induces co-existence of the human population and wildlife in our areas.

2

To help our community come out of various circles of poverty and ignorance.

3

To help the youth eradicate challenges of early marriage.

4

To retain our communities that live on the Rwenzori Mountains to eradicate environmental degradation.

5

To establish adult education Centres.

6

To enhance the capacity of poor women and men to access and share knowledge and skills on sustainable agricultural production and marketing to attain food and income security.

7

To network with other stake holders and development actors with similar objectives.

8

To promote education and advocacy programs related to tourism and environmental conservation.

9

Helping individuals and groups solving their frequent obstacles to development.

Our Activities

Conserving the Environment

We work to protect and preserve the natural ecosystem of the Rwenzori Mountains through community-based conservation efforts.

Promoting Savings and Credit Scheme

We have established a SACCO with 50 active members to promote financial inclusion and economic empowerment.

Bee Keeping

We currently have 36 beehives in different apiaries, promoting sustainable livelihoods and environmental conservation.

Promoting Tourism

We promote both community-based tourism and guided tours inside the Rwenzori Mountains National Park.

Promoting Handcraft Skills

We support local artisans in developing and marketing their handcraft products, preserving cultural heritage and providing income opportunities.

Our Achievements

We have been registered at the sub county level certificate number 22/KITH/2025.

We have established a camp site at kithakakisa with main house and a pit latrine for sanitation.

We are opening a trail deep inside the Rwenzori mountains national park which starts from kithakakisa camp site view connecting to Nyamugasana community trail, so far we have moved as far as kasusuthi 36 kilometers.

We have a reception office at Kanyatsi 2 village in Kanyatsi parish Kitholhu Sub County where we meet every Wednesday.

We have a relationship with Uganda wildlife authority (UWA)/Rwenzori mountains National park (RMNP).

We have been trained in savings and credit also how to start a SACCO by the CDO Kitholhu sub county. We have established SACCO with 50 active members.

We have been trained in bee keeping by liberty development foundation (LIDEFO). We currently have 36 beehives in different apiaries.

We have been clearing the park boundary in partnership with CCR Ihandiro park post.
