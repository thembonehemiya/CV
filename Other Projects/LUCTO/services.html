Our Services

LUCTO offers a range of services focused on eco-tourism, environmental conservation, and community development. Our initiatives aim to promote sustainable practices while providing unique experiences for visitors and opportunities for local communities.

Eco-Tourism

We offer guided tours to various attractions in and around the Rwenzori Mountains, providing authentic experiences while minimizing environmental impact.

  • Guided nature walks
  • Cultural site visits
  • Camping experiences
  • Wildlife observation
View Destinations

Environmental Conservation

We work to protect and preserve the natural environment through various conservation initiatives and community education.

  • Reforestation projects
  • Environmental education
  • Park boundary maintenance
  • Sustainable agriculture promotion
Learn More

Community Development

We support local communities through education, skills development, and economic opportunities to improve livelihoods.

  • Adult education centers
  • Skills training workshops
  • Youth empowerment programs
  • Support for local artisans

Savings and Credit Scheme

We operate a SACCO with 50 active members, providing financial services to community members to promote economic empowerment.

  • Savings accounts
  • Small business loans
  • Financial literacy training
  • Community investment opportunities
