Terms and Conditions
Booking Terms:

1. All bookings are subject to availability.

2. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking.

3. Full payment is due 14 days before the tour date.

Cancellation Policy:

1. Cancellations made more than 30 days before the tour date receive a full refund minus a 10% administration fee.

2. Cancellations made 15-30 days before the tour date receive a 50% refund.

3. Cancellations made less than 15 days before the tour date are non-refundable.

Tour Conditions:

1. Participants must follow all safety instructions provided by guides.

2. LUCTO reserves the right to modify or cancel tours due to weather conditions or other circumstances beyond our control.

3. Participants are responsible for having appropriate clothing, footwear, and personal items for their tour.

4. LUCTO is not responsible for loss or damage to personal belongings during tours.

Privacy Policy:

Your personal information will be used solely for processing your booking and will not be shared with third parties except as necessary for completing your tour arrangements.
