Inside the Park Attractions

The following attractions can be found along our 3.5km trail inside the Rwenzori Mountains National Park:

Flora

  • Amathembe
  • Smilax used for making buskers
  • Smelling trees
  • Herbal trees
  • Different species of trees
  • Different species of grass
  • Bamboo trees

Wildlife

  • Red tailed monkeys
  • Blue monkeys
  • Calabus monkeys
  • Squirrels
  • Mountain Gorillas
  • Sun birds
  • More than twenty species of birds

Landscapes

  • Valleys
  • Ridges
  • Hills
  • Eastern DR Congo view
  • Three cultural sites (Kinone, Burangwa and Katsape)
