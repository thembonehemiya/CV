Our Work

Conservation

Reforestation & Nursery Program

Our reforestation efforts focus on restoring the unique ecosystems of the Rwenzori Mountains through the establishment of tree nurseries and community planting initiatives.

Native Tree Nursery

We grow indigenous tree species that are well-adapted to the local environment and support biodiversity.

Riverbank Restoration

Planting native vegetation along riverbanks to prevent erosion and protect water sources.

Agroforestry

Promoting sustainable farming practices that integrate trees with crops and livestock.

Impact

Our nursery has produced thousands of seedlings, contributing to the restoration of degraded areas and providing sustainable income opportunities for local communities.

Sustainable Livelihoods

Community Apiary Program

Our apiary program supports biodiversity through pollination while providing sustainable income opportunities for local communities through honey production.

Beekeeping Training

We provide comprehensive training in modern beekeeping techniques to community members.

Hive Distribution

Providing beehives and equipment to trained beekeepers to establish their own apiaries.

Market Access

Helping beekeepers access markets for their honey and other bee products.

Impact

Our apiary program has successfully established community-managed beehives, resulting in increased pollination of crops and natural vegetation, while providing a sustainable source of income.

Community Development

Women's Empowerment Program

Our women's empowerment program focuses on providing young mothers with vocational skills for self-employment and financial independence.

Tailoring Skills

Training in sewing and garment making to create marketable clothing and accessories.

Handicraft Production

Teaching traditional and modern craft techniques using locally available materials.

Soap Making

Training in the production of natural soaps using local ingredients.

Future Plans

We are working to establish a dedicated training center for women's skills development, with expanded programs in mushroom growing, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship.

Education & Awareness

Community Outreach Program

Our community outreach program focuses on raising awareness about environmental conservation and sustainable practices among local communities.

School Programs

Environmental education in local schools to inspire the next generation of conservation leaders.

Community Workshops

Regular workshops on sustainable farming, waste management, and conservation practices.

Environmental Events

Organizing community events like tree planting days and clean-up campaigns.

Impact

Our outreach programs have reached thousands of community members, increasing awareness and participation in conservation efforts throughout the Rwenzori region.
