Material Donations

We welcome donations of equipment and materials to support our conservation and community programs.

Beekeeping Equipment

Protective gear, hive tools, and honey processing equipment.

Tailoring Supplies

Sewing machines, fabric, thread, and other materials for our women's training program.

Nursery Supplies

Seedling bags, watering equipment, and gardening tools for our tree nursery.
