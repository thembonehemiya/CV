Donation FAQs

RMCI is registered as a non-profit organization in Uganda. For Ugandan donors, donations are tax-deductible according to local regulations. For international donors, tax deductibility depends on your country's laws regarding foreign charitable donations. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for specific advice.

Your donation directly supports our conservation and community development programs. Approximately 85% of donations go directly to program implementation, including tree nursery operations, beekeeping equipment, vocational training materials, and community outreach activities. The remaining 15% supports essential administrative costs to ensure effective program management and reporting.

Yes, you can designate your donation for a specific program by selecting the purpose in the donation form. Options include tree planting, women's empowerment, beekeeping, and environmental education. If you have a specific project in mind that is not listed, please contact us directly to discuss your interests.

To cancel or modify your monthly donation, please email us at donations@rmci.org with your name and the changes you would like to make. You can also call us at +256 786 263 701. We will process your request within 2-3 business days and send you a confirmation.

Yes, all donors receive quarterly email updates about our programs and the impact of donations. Monthly donors also receive special reports with more detailed information about project progress. If you opt in during the donation process, you will be added to our newsletter list for regular updates about our work.
